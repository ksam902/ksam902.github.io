Completed a [Udemy course](https://www.udemy.com/the-complete-ios-game-course-using-swift/) on iOS game development using Swift 3, SpriteKit and Xcode. After completing the course, I built the beta version of an iOS game building. The limitations I had were mainly the visual assets used. I relied on free assets provided by the course and those found on the internet. The plan is to acquire custom visuals, further develop the gameplay and storyline, and release to the App Store.
