**In early 2019, there will be a new rollout of the Kent Build Your Team AUS Fantasy app. The rollout will include Men's and Women's 2019 Hockey playoffs in addtion to the AUS basketball championship.**

The AUS (Atlantic University Sport) Fantasy web application was built using the popular PHP framework Laravel, along with the Laravel service Forge and hosted on Digital Ocean. 

During the AUS Men's and Women's 2018 Basketball championship tournament, users could register and create a fantasy team. Each fantasy team consisted of 5 players from across the men's and women's teams. The web app would scape the AUS website and update the score for each fantasy team. Users could view their own fantasy teams, the tournament leaderboard, individual player profiles and each tournament game box score. 

The AUS Basketball championship concluded in March 2018; because of this, the application is no longer "live".
